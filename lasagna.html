Ingredients

  • 500g of minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g of ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups of béchamel sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the minced beef over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce. Place a layer of lasagna sheets over the sauce. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the sheets, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  6. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
